About SmartFix Repairs

Founded by Isaac Kremer, a dual major engineering student with hands-on expertise in electronics and device troubleshooting, SmartFix Repairs combines technical precision with a passion for problem-solving. We specialize in fast, reliable screen and battery replacements, serving Reading, PA with drop-off and pickup services. While phones and tablets are our core focus, our skill set extends to laptops, TVs, and household electronics—ensuring all your devices receive expert care under one roof.

Our mission is to provide transparent pricing, quality parts, and a seamless repair experience. From humble beginnings fixing personal gadgets to establishing a community-driven repair hub, SmartFix Repairs is committed to keeping your devices—and your life—running smoothly.
